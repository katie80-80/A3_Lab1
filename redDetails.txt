The MINI Cooper S Coupé is a front wheel drive motor car, with the engine mounted in the front, and a 2 door coupé body. The MINI Cooper S Coupé belongs to the R58 model family from MINI. The power is produced by a turbocharged engine of 1.6 litre capacity. This unit features double overhead camshaft valve gear, 4 cylinder layout, and 4 valves per cylinder. It produces 181 bhp (184 PS/135 kW) of power at 5500 rpm, and maximum torque of 260 N·m (192 lb·ft/26.5 kgm) at 1600 rpm. The engine delivers its power through to the wheels via a 6 speed manual gearbox.
